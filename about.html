Most days, I live somewhere between sunsets and sentences, between chai and chapters.

Hi, my name is Sentirenla and I'm so glad you're here.

I've loved fantasy and historical epics since I was old enough to turn pages. And for years, I carried a story inside me—lingering quietly at the back of my mind, waiting for its moment.
Eight months ago, I finally sat down to write Triones—a slow-burn romantic fantasy and the first in a trilogy.

When I'm not writing, you'll likely find me sketching floor plans or exploring interior design—imagining how spaces can hold emotion, just like stories do. I also love playing musical instruments, especially on quiet days when the words aren't ready yet.

Triones will be my debut novel and I can't wait to welcome you to the Kingdom of Arkotar—a realm where no one dreams.
